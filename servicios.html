Services in Tauste

Useful information about services available for residents and visitors.

Transport

Tauste has bus lines connecting with Zaragoza and nearby towns. It also has taxis and well-maintained roads to arrive by private vehicle.

  • Regular buses
  • Local taxi service
  • Public parking

Health

Basic health services available at the municipality's health center, with primary care, nursing and emergency services.

  • Tauste Health Center
  • Pharmacies
  • Basic emergencies

Education

The municipality offers early childhood, primary and secondary education centers, as well as complementary services for families.

  • Public school
  • Secondary education institute
  • Extracurricular activities

Culture

Cultural activity in Tauste includes exhibitions, cinema, workshops, children's activities and established municipal programming.

  • Municipal library
  • House of Culture
  • Events and workshops

Sports Facilities

Tauste has modern facilities for practicing sports activities.

  • Municipal swimming pools
  • Sports center
  • Padel and football courts

Tourism

Visitors can enjoy guided routes, local information points and heritage spaces open throughout the year.

  • Tourist office
  • Heritage routes
  • Visits to the Mudejar tower
