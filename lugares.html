Places of Interest in Tauste

A selection of the most representative corners and monuments of the municipality.

Church of Santa María

Mudejar-style temple declared a Site of Cultural Interest. Its tower is one of the most emblematic in the Ebro Valley.

Main Square

The nerve center of the municipality, surrounded by traditional buildings and a meeting point during celebrations and festivities.

Santa Bárbara Park

Green space ideal for leisure and relaxation, with children's play areas and sports facilities.
