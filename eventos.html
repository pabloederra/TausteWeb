Events and Festivities in Tauste

Tauste has a calendar full of tradition, culture and activities designed for all audiences.

Main Festivals

Celebrated in honor of the Virgin of Sancho Abarca, they include processions, music, shows and traditional acts.

  • The Chupinazo
  • Rosary of Crystal
  • The Rooster's Mass

Exhibitions and Culture

Throughout the year, thematic exhibitions, workshops, cinema and conferences are held at the Casa de Cultura.

  • Monthly programming at the Casa de Cultura
  • Workshops
  • Cinema and theater

Traditional Market

The San Bartolomé neighborhood periodically hosts a market with local products, crafts and typical gastronomy.

  • Local products
  • Crafts
  • Tastings

Pilgrimages

Local pilgrimages bring together neighbors and visitors to enjoy a festive atmosphere between tradition and culture.

Dancers of Tauste

The Dancers of Tauste perform various shows throughout the year, highlighting their participation in the Main Festivals.

Municipal Band Concerts

The Tauste Municipal Band offers concerts and performances at different events throughout the year.
