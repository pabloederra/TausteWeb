Gastronomy of Tauste

The traditional cuisine of Tauste combines historical recipes, local products and flavors that reflect the essence of Aragon.

Typical Dishes

Rabbit Fritón

One of the most emblematic dishes of the area prepared with rabbit, garlic, paprika and olive oil.

Traditional Migas

Humble and delicious dish, prepared with bread, oil and accompaniments such as grapes, egg or sausage.

Fullatre

A type of cake unique to the area. They are an essential part of the festive gastronomy of the municipality.

Recommended Restaurants

  • Asador Candeque: Aragonese cuisine with specialties from the area.
  • La Loma'l Cuco: ideal for meats, tapas and traditional menus.
  • Taberna Ñai: varied tapas and local atmosphere in the center.

Highlighted Local Products

The region offers quality products recognized in Aragon:
